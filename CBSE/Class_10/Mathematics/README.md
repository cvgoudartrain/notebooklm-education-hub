### Suggested Prompts for NotebookLM
Use these prompts when interacting with the AI notebooks:

**Concept Clarity:**
- "Explain the Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic with a real-life analogy"
- "What's the difference between AP and GP? Give examples"
- "Explain Pythagoras theorem proof step by step"

**Exam Preparation:**
- "List 5 important questions from this chapter that appear frequently in CBSE exams"
- "Create a 10-question practice test with increasing difficulty"
- "What are common mistakes students make in this chapter?"

**Quick Revision:**
- "Summarize all formulas in this chapter"
- "Create a mind map of key concepts"
- "List the most important theorems I need to memorize"
